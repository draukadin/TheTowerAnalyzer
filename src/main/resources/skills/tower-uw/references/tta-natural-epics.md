# Natural Epics Reference

Unique abilities are only present on **natural epics** (modules drawn directly as Epic or higher from the gacha). Fodder modules never gain these abilities regardless of rarity.

Scaling values shown are for the `[x]` placeholder in each effect description.

---

## Cannon Modules

| Module | Effect | Epic | Legendary | Mythic | Ancestral |
|:-------|:-------|:-----|:----------|:-------|:----------|
| Astral Deliverance (AD) | Bounce shot's range is increased by 3% of tower's total range. Each bounce increases the projectile's damage by [x]%. | 20% | 40% | 60% | 80% |
| Being Annihilator (BA) | When you super crit, your next [x] attacks are guaranteed super crits. | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 |
| Death Penalty (DP) | Chance of [x]% to mark an enemy for death when it spawns, causing the first hit to destroy it. | 5% | 8% | 11% | 15% |
| Havoc Bringer (HB) | [x]% chance for rend armor to instantly go to max. | 10% | 13% | 15% | 20% |
| Shrink Ray (SR) | Attacks have a 1% chance to apply a non-stacking effect that decreases the enemy's mass by [x]%. | 10% | 20% | 30% | 40% |
| Amplifying Strike (AS) | Killing a boss or elite enemy increases Tower damage by 5x for [x]s. | 5s | 11s | 18s | 26s |
