## Full Description

```
The Tower Analyzer is the Android companion app for the The Tower Analyzer desktop application. It gives you the fastest, simplest way to get your battle reports off your phone and into the cloud so the desktop app can consume them for tracking and analysis.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Finish a run and open the battle report from the battle history
2. Click the copy to clipboard button on the battle report
3. Open The Tower Analyzer, select your run type.
4. Tap Submit.

That's all — your report is delivered to the cloud automatically.

SUPPORTED RUN TYPES
• Farming
• Tournament
• Milestone
• Event
• Dissonance (Attack, Defense, Utility, Ultimate Weapon)

REGIONAL COVERAGE
Reports are routed through the regional endpoint you select for fast, reliable delivery:
• US East (Ohio)
• EU West (Ireland)
• Asia Pacific (Tokyo)

FIRST-TIME SETUP
You will be asked for your Player ID once when you first open the app. Find it in The Tower app under Settings — there is a copy button next to it so you can paste it directly. For a full setup walkthrough, see: [SETUP_VIDEO_URL]
```
