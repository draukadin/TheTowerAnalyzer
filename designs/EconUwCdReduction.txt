### Situation
* The cool downs of GT/BH/DW are 180 seconds
* The cool downs of Golden Bot and Summon are 90 Seconds
* GB and Summon are in a 2:1 sync with GT/BH/DW

### GT/BH/DW Cool Down reduction to 170 seconds
* New GB and Summon CD: 85 * 2 = 170
* There are two options to get the Golden Bot Cooldown reduced by 5 seconds
  * Option 1 use Medals to get reduce by 3 seconds and two lab levels to reduce by 2 seconds
  * Option 2 use five lab levels to reduce by 5 seconds
* Why not just use medals? The medal levels only reduce in increments of three so two medal levels would reduce the CD to 84 seconds

Here is the breakdown of the pros and cons for both options to lower your Golden Bot cooldown to 85 seconds, keeping it in a perfect 2:1 sync with your newly reduced 170-second Ultimate Weapon cooldowns.

---

## Option 1: The Hybrid Approach (Medals + Labs)

This option splits the 5-second reduction by using 460 medals for a 3-second drop and 2 lab levels for the remaining 2 seconds.

* **Pros:**
* **Massive Time Savings:** It requires only **18 hours and 15 minutes** of lab research time. This gets your sync online incredibly fast compared to grinding out days of lab work.
* **Precision Targeting:** Because medals only reduce cooldowns in 3-second increments, combining a single medal buy with labs allows you to hit the exact 85-second target. Relying entirely on medals would overshoot to 84 seconds and break your clean 2:1 sync.

* **Cons:**
* **High Opportunity Cost for Medals:** Spending **460 medals** is a significant investment  that diverts resources away from upgrading other bot stats (like range) or buying event themes.

---

## Option 2: The Pure Lab Approach

This option bypasses medals entirely and relies on grinding out 5 full lab levels to achieve the 5-second reduction.

* **Pros:**
* **Zero Medal Cost:** This saves you **460 medals** , leaving your event currency entirely free to be spent on other progression milestones or bot upgrades.

* **Cons:**
* **Extended Lab Bottleneck:** You will tie up a lab slot for **2 days, 10 hours, and 16 minutes**. This delays your economic sync and prevents that lab from working on other crucial research.

### The 1.5-Run Transition Window
During those ~14.5 hours of lab work, your alignment will look like this:
* **GT / BH / DW:** Perfectly synced at 170 seconds (Instant Stone upgrade).
* **Summon:** Perfectly synced at 85 seconds (Instant Bits upgrade).
* **Golden Bot:** Temporarily at 90 seconds, drifting slightly against your new 170s/85s cycle.

### Why the Temporary Desync is Worth It
* **Negligible Coin Loss:** Golden Bot drifting for just 1.5 runs is a microscopic blip in your overall lifetime coin progression.
* **Massive Medal Payoff:** In exchange for that tiny, temporary efficiency dip, you permanently pocket **460 medals**. You can instantly dump those saved medals into Golden Bot's **Range** or **Bonus**, which will permanently multiply your earnings across *all* future runs.

---

### Recommended Execution Order

To make the transition as seamless as possible, you can line up the upgrades in this sequence:

1. **Queue the Labs:** Dedicate a lab slot to the Golden Bot cooldown levels and ensure your $4\times$ cell speed-up is active.
2. **Drop the Stones & Bits:** Upgrade your UW cooldowns and the Summon cooldown simultaneously to lock in their new 2:1 sync immediately.
3. **The Interim Runs:** Knock out your ~1.5 farming runs while the lab grinds away.
4. **Lock the Sync:** The moment those lab levels finish, Golden Bot drops to 85 seconds, and your entire economic engine snaps perfectly back into harmony.
